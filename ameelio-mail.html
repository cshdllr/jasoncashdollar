Ameelio Connect

Ameelio Connect is a product where people can schedule video calls, voice calls, and message with their incarcerated loved ones.

Ameelio Messaging

Messsaging was a very important part of the product because it allowed people to stay connected at times when they were not able to make phone calls. It also reduced the friction associated with staying in touch because you no longer needed to schedule a call and wait for approval.
