Chrono

Chrono is a daily history game that I built with a friend. We were inspired by Wordle to make something that brought people together for a few moments each day. We saw the focus on history as a good way to create interesting topics for conversation and to learn while you played the game. Did you know the first use of the word "robot" to describe mechanical people was in 1920?
