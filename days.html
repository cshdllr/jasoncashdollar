DAYS

DAYS is a side project I built for iOS. It's a simple app that helps you celebrate hidden milestones by tracking meaningful dates from your life. Add any date that matters to you—your first day at a new job, when you met someone special, the day you adopted your pet—and DAYS shows you how much time has passed in delightfully unexpected ways.

Discover that you've lived through 47 full moons since starting college, celebrated 50,000 hours since your wedding day, or just hit your 10,000th day of being alive. These hidden milestones are happening all around us. DAYS makes sure you don't miss them.
