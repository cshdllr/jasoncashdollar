Facebook fundraisers

People have raised over $2 billion dollars for important causes using Facebook's fundraising tools.

Here are some memorable projects from my time on the team.

Fundraiser Center

A place for people to browse fundraisers for causes they are interested in.

Fundraiser Creation

Designed a much simpler creation experience that allowed people to get a better preview of their fundraiser before it was published.

Giving Tuesday

A series of designs that elevated fundraising on Giving Tuesday, leading to $120 million dollars raised by the Facebook community on a single day in 2019.
