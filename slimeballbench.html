SlimeBallBench

SlimeBallBench is a benchmark where AI models play each other in a game of slime soccer. Each model controls a player, reacting in real time to the ball and its opponent, and the games play out as short, watchable matches.

I built it because most AI benchmarks are static tables of numbers. Slime soccer turns the comparison into something you can actually watch: two models, one ball, and a winner. The site tracks videos, standings, and stats across matchups.
