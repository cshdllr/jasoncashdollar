Running

Running has been a big part of my life since about 2014. Here are some of my favorite races and runs.

Four Pass Loop

An iconic loop in Colorado! One of the most beautiful runs I've ever done.

Boston Marathon

I never thought I'd have the opportunity to run this race. It was a very special experience even though I didn't run as well as I had hoped.

CIM

I avoided road marathons for years, but at some point wanted to try again. I'm glad I did because running under 3 hours was a huge accomplishment for me.

Broken Arrow

One of my favorite races in California. I ran the triple crown one year, which consists of 3 different raced 3 days in a row. I still think it's the most climbing I've ever done in a single week.

The North Face 50

Similar to the Quad Dipsea, I felt excited to be able to run on the trails that I spent so much time on.

Quad Dipsea

I love/hate this trail so much. A classic in California and ground zero of where I really started loving trail running.

AR 50

My first 50 mile race. I remember starting the race soaking wet at 4am and the true happiness of finishing and laying on the ground.

Way Too Cool

A very muddy race that I finished and didn't feel totally broken.

Napa Valley 50k

My first ultra marathon, which I ran with my friend Barry. Lost a lot of toenails due to this run.
